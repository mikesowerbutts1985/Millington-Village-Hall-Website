Accommodation

Camping-barn style stays - simple, warm, and practical.

We offer basic facilities for overnight accommodation: a well-equipped kitchen; indoor toilets; electric heating; hot water and drying rack; and a newly fitted electric shower.

Well-equipped kitchen

Perfect for groups - prepare meals, tea/coffee, and breakfast on-site.

Sleeping space

The hall can provide up to 10 camp beds or sleeping mats in a single room.

Charges

£15.00 per person per night for a bed and minimal breakfast (cereal & coffee/tea), or £20 for a bed and full English self-cook breakfast plus cereal, toast etc.

Booking

You are advised to contact 01759 301931, or email accommodation@millingtonvillagehall.org.uk to ensure the date you want is available.

Food nearby

You can dine at The Gait Inn most nights, and the Ramblers’ Rest tea rooms are open for lunches Thursday to Monday (and dinner on application).

Tip for walkers

With major walking routes nearby, this is a great overnight base. See Around Millington.
