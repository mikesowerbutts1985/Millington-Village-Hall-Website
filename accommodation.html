Accommodation

Camping-barn style stays - simple, warm, and practical.

We offer basic facilities for overnight accommodation: a well-equipped kitchen; indoor toilets; electric heating; hot water and drying rack; and a newly fitted electric shower.

Well-equipped kitchen

Perfect for groups - prepare meals, tea/coffee, and breakfast on-site.

Sleeping space

The hall can provide up to 10 camp beds or sleeping mats in a single room.

Charges

Overnight stay rate £25 per person, per night and includes a cereal breakfast, tea and coffee. For additional supply of a big breakfast to include sausage, bacon, egg, beans/tomato, bread, butter and jam is £8 per person. Self catered.


Please state your requested date(s) to stay and and breakfast requirements or preferences when booking, either by phone or email.


Hourly hire rate is £10 per hour. Millington Parish residents discount applies, please ask the Booking Secretary for details.
